The Environmental Crisis

Pollution

Plastic bags take hundreds of years to decompose, clogging our waterways and contaminating the soil we rely on for food.

Animal Harm

Wildlife and marine life frequently mistake plastic for food, leading to thousands of preventable deaths every year.

Microplastics

As plastic breaks down, it enters our water and food systems as microplastics, posing serious risks to human health.

Our Sustainable Vision

100% Biodegradable

Banana fiber decomposes naturally in just a few months, returning nutrients back to the earth.

Empowering Farmers

By utilizing agricultural waste (banana stems), we create a new revenue stream for local farming communities.

Superior Strength

Unlike standard paper, banana fiber is naturally strong and moisture-resistant, making it perfect for reusable bags.
